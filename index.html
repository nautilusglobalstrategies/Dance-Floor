FAQ

These are the questions people will ask before they book.

Can customers pay a deposit now and the rest later?

Yes, the site should support deposit, partial, and full payment options.

Can this be hosted on GitHub?

Yes, the website front end can live on GitHub Pages, while payments connect through a checkout service.

Can it look like a party brand instead of a plain service site?

Yes, by using strong photography, dark backgrounds, bright accents, and a simple, high-energy layout.
